Our Heritage & Calling

About MFC KIM Church

"A family of believers united by faith, sustained by grace, and dedicated to community stewardship."

Our History & Purpose

Rooted in Faith, Growing in Love

Founded on the enduring pillars of faith, prayer, and community stewardship, MFC KIM has been a beacon of hope and spiritual heritage for generations.

We believe in building strong families, discipling believers of all ages, and extending compassionate outreach to those in need. Our worship combines reverent traditional hymnody with heartfelt, spirit-led praise in an atmosphere of warmth and grace.

100% Free Community Focused
Our Calling

Mission, Vision & Core Beliefs

We stand firmly on the infallible truth of God's Word as our guide for worship, discipleship, and service.

🔥

Our Mission

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, discipling believers toward maturity, and serving our community with sacrificial love and humility.

🌟

Our Vision

To be a sanctuary of peace and spiritual heritage where individuals and families experience genuine conversion, biblical transformation, and enduring fellowship.

📖

Core Scriptural Beliefs

We affirm the Divine authority of Scripture, the Trinity, salvation by grace through faith alone, and the calling of every believer to holy stewardship and prayer.
